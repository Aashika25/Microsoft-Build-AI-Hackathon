People Group Program Management
This page provides an overview of Program Management within the People Group today.
Overview
Program Managers in the People Group drive alignment, measurement, and execution of our most impactful business objectives and deliverables. They keep our division organized, on track and connected through effective project management while maintaining agility and cross-functional collaboration.
Key Competencies
Regardless of the team, our Program Management key competencies are consistent to ensure the function at its core remains aligned. While the job families remain the
SSOT
for roles across GitLab, a few key competencies and behaviors to expect from all Program Managers within the People Group are:
Key Competency
Behavior
Subject matter expertise
Guides programs within their functional area
Effective communication
Provide regular project status updates and project visibility to stakeholders
Growth mindset
Drive continuous improvement through retrospectives and optimization
Change management
Facilitate pivots and timeline adjustments effectively
Stakeholder management
Keep teams aligned and accountable for executing deliverables
Strategic planning
Ensures work aligns with the division’s and organization’s overall goals
Program methodologies
Document processes, manage project plans and mitigate risks

Collaboration Framework
When multiple Program Managers work on the same project:
The Program Manager from the DRI team leads end-to-end management
Other Program Managers partner from their team’s prospective
Close Collaboration ensures unified execution
