Modern Health
Information on GitLab’s Employee Assistance Program.
Mental Wellness Support
GitLab provides comprehensive mental wellness support through Modern Health to help you navigate life’s challenges with confidence. Whether you’re managing everyday stress, working through career transitions, or supporting your family’s wellbeing, our partnership gives you access to professional coaching, therapy, and digital wellness resources designed to support you at every stage of life.

Key Features
Professional Support That Fits Your Needs
6 complimentary coaching sessions per year for personal and professional growth
6 complimentary therapy sessions per year with licensed mental health professionals
Unlimited digital wellness programs including guided meditations and self-paced courses
Live group sessions (Circles) led by Modern Health professionals
24/7 access to curated content and resources
Comprehensive Coverage Areas
Modern Health supports you through work performance, relationships, stress and anxiety management, healthy lifestyles, financial wellbeing, diversity and inclusion, life challenges, and mindfulness practices.

Understanding Your Options
Coaching vs. Therapy - What’s Right for You?
Coaching
focuses on personal and professional development for individuals looking to:
Develop leadership or communication skills
Navigate career transitions
Build healthy habits
Improve work-life balance
Learn more about professional coaching
in this 42-second video.
Therapy
provides clinical support from licensed professionals for individuals experiencing:
Persistent anxiety or depression
Symptoms affecting daily functioning
Clinical mental health concerns
Need for specialized treatment approaches
Not sure which is right for you? The wellbeing assessment will help determine the most appropriate starting point, and you can always request therapy services by contacting
help@joinmodernhealth.com
.
Important Details
Benefit Reset & Usage
Benefits reset annually on March 20
Unused sessions do not roll over
Sessions can be shared with family members (each counts toward your total)
24-hour cancellation policy applies
Remember: GitLab PTO policy and non-linear workday structure support you in making time for mental health appointments

Privacy & Confidentiality
Your privacy matters. GitLab does not receive individual usage data or know whether you’re using Modern Health services. All conversations with coaches and therapists are confidential, with standard exceptions for safety concerns as required by law.
