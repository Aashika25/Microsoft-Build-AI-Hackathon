Everyone can contribute
At GitLab our goal is that everyone can contribute. This applies to GitLab team members and the wider community through community contributions. We welcome contributions to any and all features, but recognize that first time contributors may prefer to start with smaller features. To support this we maintain a list of
quick wins
that may be more suitable for first time contributors, and contributors new to the domains in Software Supply Chain Security.
If the contributor needs an EE license, we can point towards the
Contributing to the GitLab Enterprise Edition (EE)
section on the Community contributors workflows page.
